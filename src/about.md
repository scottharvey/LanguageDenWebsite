## Why one person?

Because it keeps things honest. There's no marketing team inflating feature promises, no growth team pushing engagement mechanics that aren't good for you. Just me, using the app myself every day, fixing what's broken and building what's actually useful.

It also means things move at a measured pace. I'd rather ship something solid than something fast.
